The Certified Tableau CRM and Einstein Discovery Consultant certification course covered the core principles of Salesforce's advanced analytics platform, including how to design, implement, and optimize solutions using Tableau CRM (formerly Einstein Analytics) and Einstein Discovery. Participants learned how to connect data from multiple sources, model and prepare datasets, and build interactive dashboards that provide actionable insights for business stakeholders. The course emphasized best practices in security, sharing, and governance to ensure scalable and compliant analytics solutions.

In the context of predictive analytics, the course addressed how Einstein Discovery delivers AI-driven recommendations by applying machine learning models directly within the Salesforce ecosystem. Students practiced building, training, and deploying predictive models, explored how to interpret key drivers and insights, and integrated predictions into workflows and dashboards to support data-driven decision making.

By the end of the course, attendees gained hands-on experience in creating end-to-end analytics applications, from data preparation through visualization and prediction. Earning certification validated their ability to design and deploy Tableau CRM dashboards and Einstein Discovery solutions that deliver measurable business value.
