Salesforce Certified Associate

I completed the Salesforce Certified Associate course and demonstrated foundational proficiency across the Salesforce ecosystem, including Customer 360 concepts, navigation, core data model, and reports & dashboards.

Through the training, I applied knowledge of Salesforce best practices and platform terminology, practiced navigating apps to locate and manage records, and built and interpreted basic reports and dashboards. I also gained awareness of user administration and sharing at a foundational level.

  • Ecosystem & Roles: How organizations use Salesforce and where different roles fit.
  • Navigation: Finding data, using search, and moving between apps, tabs, and records.
  • Data Model: Objects, fields, relationships, and record basics.
  • Reports & Dashboards: Creating and reading essential analytics.

This learning path is ideal for newcomers and professionals exploring program or project management, product management, consulting, and related roles where Salesforce literacy is valuable.
